Our Consulting Model

At Spotlight, we understand that needs can vary from consulting assistance to long-term hiring. We support both through our proven success in delivering skill and culture-matched talent through a flexible model that’s easy to understand.

Consulting

When your goal is to accelerate product delivery, fill a skills gap temporarily, or assemble a team to knock out a project, we can support you with the talent you need fast.

Consulting to Permanent

If you're interested in hiring someone to join your team for the long haul, we allow you to evaluate the impact of a potential hire with our consult-to-perm model. We make it simple and affordable to convert our consultants to full-time employees after a 6-month period.
